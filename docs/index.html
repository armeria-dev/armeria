Armeria is a Multi-User Dungeon, also commonly referred to as a MUD. It is accessible through the use of our custom-built web client using any modern device with a web browser.

Starting out on Aluxium, a medium-sized planet, explore the various environments that dot the landscape all around the globe. From gigantic mega-cities in constant motion, to peaceful rural farmland, Aluxium has everything you could ever need. This is, in large part, thanks to Armeria Industries (AI).
